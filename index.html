The Right Anaesthetic for Your Procedure

We understand that different procedures and treatments call for different anaesthetic formulations. That’s why we offer a wide range of anaesthetics, including:

Prilocaine: Prilocaine is a commonly used local anaesthetic that provides excellent numbing effects, making it suitable for various procedures.

Benzocaine: Benzocaine is known for its fast-acting pain relief properties, making it ideal for quick procedures like tattooing.

Lignocaine (Lidocaine): Lignocaine, also known as lidocaine, is a versatile anaesthetic that can be used for a wide range of medical and cosmetic treatments.

Tetracaine: Tetracaine is a potent anaesthetic that is often used in combination with other numbing agents for more complex procedures.

Epinephrine: Tailoring Your Medication

Depending on the nature of your treatment, the equipment used, the area of your body being treated, and the duration of the procedure, we may recommend adding epinephrine to your anaesthetic formulation. Epinephrine can help prolong the numbing effect and reduce bleeding, ensuring a smoother experience.
